\section{Pickups on an electric guitar}
One of the most important parts of an electric guitar, is the transducer which converts the strings vibrations into an electrical signal. This is done with pickups, which as seen in \autoref{fig:guitar_parts}, is placed on the body. Typically an electric guitar has two or three pickups and the type and combination of pickups can vary. Two types of pickups are the single coil pickups and the dual coil (Humbucker) pickups.
